A life well lived on and off the court

Jeyakumar Tharmalingam — known to all of us as Mahinthan — was born on March 6, 1973. For decades, he was a constant and beloved presence within the Canadian Tamil Volleyball Association. Whether it was a weekend tournament, a weeknight league game, or simply a gathering of friends at the court, Mahinthan was there.

His sudden passing on August 9, 2025, left a profound void in our community. He was 52 years old, full of life, and still actively playing the sport he loved. Those who played alongside him carry memories of his laughter, his competitive spirit, and the generosity he showed to players of all ages and abilities.

Mahinthan understood what CTVA is really about — not just volleyball, but belonging. He understood that these courts have always been a place where our Tamil-Canadian community comes to feel at home. He was part of why they felt that way.

We hold his memory with deep gratitude and immense love. May he rest in eternal peace.

❀
